Our Story

Isaac Eco-Bamboo Art creates high-quality, customizable bamboo bags that offer a sustainable alternative to plastic and chemically treated leather. With over two decades of experience, we've perfected the art of bamboo crafting, creating pieces that not only beautify spaces but also contribute to a sustainable future.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, high-quality, and customizable bamboo bags as a sustainable alternative to plastic and leather products.

Our Vision

To be a leading brand in eco-friendly fashion accessories, driving sustainability and local craftsmanship in Ghana and beyond.

Meet the Master Craftsman

Isaac Kyereda

is a passionate innovator dedicated to sustainable craftsmanship. Frustrated by the lack of affordable eco-friendly bags, he started Issac Eco-Bamboo Art to create high-quality, customizable bamboo bags that reduce plastic waste and support local artisans.

Each piece crafted by Isaac reflects his commitment to sustainability and his deep understanding of bamboo's natural properties.
